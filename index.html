A Traveler's Guide to the City of Lights!

The largest city in France and its capital, Paris is one of the most prestigious global centers for art, architecture, fashion and cuisine. It attracts millions of tourists each year from all over the globe and is well known for its iconic structures and world class museums - not to mention the delicious food. Whatever your interests may be, there is always something for you to enjoy in this diverse and multideminsonal city; with this guide, you won't miss out on any of the city's biggest attractions and can be guaranteed a magical experience in the City of Lights.
